Morroco Traditons

Morroccans prefer to eat with their hands, as they are considered cleaner. Traditionally, food is taken only by the right hand, with three fingers. Bowls of water are prepared for washing hands before and after meals.
A Morrocan Holiday punctuated by commemorations of the day of the king's formal accession to the throne.Military parades, fireworks and lively street festivities.
Morocco Independence Day, celebrated on November 18th each year, commemorates the recovery of independence and the end of the French and Spanish protectorates in Morocco on November 18, 1956.Parades take place across Morocco to recognize the country's long fight for freedom.
