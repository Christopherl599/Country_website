The Beauty of Morroco

Summary of Morroco

Morocco is a country where African, Arab and European cultures are mixed. Located at the mouth of the Mediterranean Sea, Morocco is the closest point of contact to Europe for Africa . Morocco is a land of tolerance, a blend of cultures and religions, a country known for its , art, food, music and architecture makes Morocco an unforgettable destination.
